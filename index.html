A modular tyre system

With reTyre you can instantly change your tyre attributes and design using reTyre skins.

  • reTyre One, a fast rolling base tyre with integrated zip-on technology
  • reTyre One, a fast rolling base tyre with integrated zip-on technology

reTyre base tyre

The base tyre has integrated zippers on each side and performs as a conventional tyre, meaning it can be used all year without Skins. The tyre is built for durability and low rolling resistance, and its integrated zippers has been shown to have a lifespan equal to or greater than the tyre itself.

reTyre Skins

The modular system currently have 5 types of Skins available for each base tyre size. The Skins are easily mounted on top of the tyre in less than 60 seconds without the need of any tool.

Better biking experience

Correct treading provides better grip, less rolling resistance and a better biking experience. The tyres also last longer.

Simple & flexible

Attach a new skin whenever needed. Adapt to unpredictable weather and road conditions – year-round.

60 second installation

No special skills or tools necessary. If you can use a zipper, you can use reTyre!

Grab & Go

Be ready for what’s around the next turn or after work. reTyre skins are easy to bring along.

Our business model

reTyre business model incorporate two distribution models which both outperforms that of conventional tyre brands as they fully utilize the aftermarket potential:

  1. 1. Distribution of both tyres and Skins through existing distribution channels (OEM/retail)
  2. 2. Distribution of tyres through OEM and reTyre exclusive distribution of Skins (DTC)
  • We secure the initial growth by utilizing the whole value chain and its existing distribution channels as seen to the right. This means that retailers are allowed to sell our Skins.
  • Simultaneously we already position ourselves for transition to a DTC business model with Kickback as explained previously.

Our Distribution Channels

  • Direct to consumer

    With sales online we have no intermediaries and therefore increase our margin This also makes it possible for us to reduce our retail price by up to 25 and still make 4 x as much as we would through retail chains.

  • Retail chains (AM and OEM)

    Retail chains often have their own in house brands of bikes e g XXL’s White models, 60 000 yearly) We initially provide Base Tyres for aftermarket (as an entry into the chain As the retail chain gain experience with our base tyres we transition to OEM, giving their bikes a sales advantage In addition to this the in house brand will get the additional revenue stream of our kickback model.

  • Reaching bike workshops

    In new markets we are well positioned to enter through distributors. In many cases distributors have easy access to bike workshops who provide tyre change and bike service. These workshops are positioned to get our tyres onto bikes as an upgrade option during service and we offer them a high margin on the tyres as well as marketing assistance.

  • OEM to bike brands

    For small brands 15 000 bikes yearly) with own distribution channels we initiate with a free pilot These brands have short decision timelines and our tyres gives their bikes unique qualities and improves their sales, and our Kickback on Skins give them added revenue which will be significant for them.

Our unique opportunity

The problem with the tyre is that there are multiple steps between brand and end user.

  • One time transactional sales to the vehicle manufacturer (that covers 85 of the volumes)
  • Limited end-customer data with this model, losing all means of communicating directly with the user
  • This makes each brand in the industry easily replaceable and uniquely vulnerable to competition in price and margin

Disruptive technology

  • Due to patented modular technology, reTyre is the only tyre company that can address this problem with a unique solution
  • Introducing a "Nespresso-alike" model and licensing, reTyre can add value to each actor of the value chain!
  • Due to technology limitations of "regular" bicycle tyre brands, reTyre model will assure major competitive advantage
  • We offer our partners A NEW REVENUE STREAM

What are the advantages

  • Unique recurring revenue for both reTyre and the Bike Brand throughout the lifetime of the tyre
  • No extra work required for the Bike Brand, no aftersales, stocking cost, marketing cost or customer service needed
  • This paired with the offered price on the base tyres, makes reTyre the best OEM option for any bike brand
  • A reTyre user buys on a low average 4-7 Skins through the 6 year lifespan of the base tyre
  • If a brand spec 100 000 bicycles with reTyre they can expect a potential total kickback of 1 500 000 EURO, without any further efforts

What is Kickback?

  • reTyre's own "Nespresso" business model with a unique feature of revenue sharing with OEM partners
  • Bike brands distribute the base tyres («coffee machines») through their retail network
  • reTyre exclusively sells Skins (»coffee capsules») to customers and transfer a set percentage of the revenue back to the brand!
